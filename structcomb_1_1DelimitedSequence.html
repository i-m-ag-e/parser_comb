Detailed Description

template<ParseRule P, ParseRule Delim, bool AllowZero>
struct comb::DelimitedSequence< P, Delim, AllowZero >

Combinator that executes a parser as many times as it succeeds, separated by a delimiter.

Allocates on the heap and not constexpr compatible before C++20.

Usage given with construction functions comb::separated_by0 and comb::separated_by1.

Template Parameters
Pparser to execute
Delimparser that parses the delimiter
AllowZeroflag to allow or disallow zero executions of a parser
