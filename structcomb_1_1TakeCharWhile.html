template<Predicate< char > Fn, bool AllowEmpty>
struct comb::TakeCharWhile< Fn, AllowEmpty >

Parser that consumes characters while they match a predicate, returning the matched substring.

Usage given with construction functions take_char_while0 and take_char_while1.
