◆ ParseResult

template<typename T >
using comb::ParseResult = typedef std::optional<std::pair<T, std::string_view> >

Resulting type of all parsers.

Currently, there is no support for having error messages or maintaining states across parsers. So, the parsers just return a std::nullopt on failure, or a T wrapped in std::optional on success.
