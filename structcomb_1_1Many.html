Detailed Description

template<ParseRule P, bool AllowZero>
struct comb::Many< P, AllowZero >

Combinator that executes a parser as many times as it succeeds.

Since this returns a std::vector of the produced values, this allocates on the heap. This is also not constexpr compatible before C++20.

Usage given with construction functions comb::zero_or_more and comb::one_or_more.

AllowZeroflag to allow or disallow zero executions of a parser
